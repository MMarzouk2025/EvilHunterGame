A long time ago before humans inhabit the earth demons were living there. They were showing off their unlimited power to each other and that power had transformed them to the dark side.
They started to doom every living thing and with that kind of chaos
angels were cast out of heaven to the earth with one mission "cleaning that mess". After centuries of war and destruction, humans started to dwell the earth and the power of the dark side were growing wherefore angels waited for a help from the one who the prophecy has mentioned.
Till that day which the one came and allied with angels to defeat the dark side and rescue the earth by bringing demons back to hell with no return and they named themselves as The Order of The Death.
